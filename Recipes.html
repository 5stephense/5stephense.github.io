      "Creammy Watermelon Smoothy

      Ingreedients

      • "2 cups chopped seedless watermelon plus watermelon wedges for garnish"
      • "1 small banana (about five ounces"
      • "3/4 cup fresh strawberries hulled and halved)"
      • "1/2 cup unsweatened coconut-milk yogart
      • "2 teaspoons agave syrup or honey"
      • "1/8 teaspoon salt"

      Directions

      • "1. Arrange chopped watermelon on a bakingsheet lined with parchment paper and freeze until completely frozen, (at least 2 hours or up to 12 hours)"
      • "2. Cut banana in half crosswise. Peel one banana half and set aside. Reserve the other banana half for another use. Place the frozen watermelon, peeled banana half, strawberries, yogart, agave or honey, and salt in a blender. Process until completely smooth and creamy, (about 1 minute stopping to scrape down sides as needed). Devide between two glasses and garnish with watermelon slices if desired."

      "Cherry-mocha Smoothy"

      Ingredients

      • "1 cup frozen pitted dark-sweet cherries"
      • "1 cup unsweetened chocolate almond milk"
      • "5.3 to 6 ounce carton fat-free vanilla Greek yogart"
      • "1/2 medium banana"
      • "2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder"
      • "2 tablespoons almondbutter"
      • "1 teaspoon instant espresso coffee powder
      • "1 teaspoon vanilla"
      • "2 cups ice cubes"
      • "1 tablespoon dark chocolate shavings, chocolate-covered espresso beans"

      Directions

      • "1. In a blender, combine the cherries, almond milk, Greek yogart, banana, cocoa powder, almondbutter, espresso coffee powder, and vanilla. Cover and blend until smooth, add ice cubes, cover and blend until smooth. Pour into two glasses and if desired, top with dark chocolate shavings, chocolate-covered espresso beans, and/or additional banana slices."

      "Cherry Berry Oatmeal Smoothy"

      Ingredients

      • "1/2 cup water"
      • "1/3 cup quick cooking rolled oats"
      • "1/2 cup lite almond milk or fat-free milk"
      • 3/4 cup fresh or frozenunsweetened strawberries, (partially thawed)"
      • "1/2 cup fresh or frozen unsweetened pitted dark-sweet cherries, (partially thawed)"
      • "1-2 tablespoons almondbutter"
      • "1 tablespoon honey"
      • "1/2 cup small ice cubes"

      Directions

      • "1. In a medium bowl, combine water and oats. Microwave for one minute. Stir in 1/4 cup of the milk. Microwave 30-50 seconds more or until oats are vary tender. Cool for five minutes."
      • 2. In a blender, combine the oat mixture the remaining 1/4 cup of the milk, and the next four ingredients through honey. Cover and blend until smooth. Add ice cubes, cover and blend until smooth. Top with additional fruit if desired."
